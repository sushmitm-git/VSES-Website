About Vidyasagar Arts College

Vidyasagar Kala Mahavidyalaya was formally opened on 9th August, 1999 at Khairi (Bijewada), a small tribal village at the outskirts of Ramtek. The college is recognized and fully aided by the Government of Maharashtra and permanently affiliated to RTMNU, Nagpur.

Founded by the visionary Late Dr. Vinodkumar Jaiswal, the institution focuses on delivering quality higher education to tribal and rural communities in and around Ramtek.
