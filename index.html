Institutional Milestones

  • 1998: Establishment of Vidyasagar Education Society, Ramtek.

  • August 1999: Est. of Vidyasagar Kala Mahavidyalaya Khairi (Bijewada).

  • 2002: Introduction of N.S.S. Unit.

  • 2003: 1st Gold Medal in University Convocation (17 medals since).

  • 2006: Introduction of YCMOU Centre.

  • 2014: Shifted to own building & Campus; Permanent Affiliation to RTMNU.

  • 2015: Included under section 2(f) and 12(B) of UGC Act.

  • 2018: Accredited with 'B' Grade (CGPA 2.20) by NAAC.

  • 2021-22: PG Courses in Marathi & Sociology.

  • 2022-23: Construction of Auditorium & Building Expansion.

  • 2024-25: Late Dr. Vinodkumaraji Jaiswal Nursing Institute.

  • 2025-26: Vidyasagar Police Academy & Competitive Exam Center.

Library

The college has a well-furnished and fully computerized Library that serves as a vibrant hub of learning and research. It houses a rich collection of more than 2,896 books along with a variety of periodicals, journals of national and international repute, and newspapers in Marathi, Hindi, and English.

Equipped with INFLIBNET software, the library provides access to extensive online academic resources and digital databases. An adjoining Knowledge Resource Centre with ten computers and Wi-Fi connectivity enables students to explore e-resources, prepare projects, and engage in online learning activities.

The library continuously strives to promote reading culture, research orientation, and academic excellence among students and faculty members.
